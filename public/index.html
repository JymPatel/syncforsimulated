sync for simulated

current version1.1

utilizing sync-for-simulated you can sync your stats from simcompanies.com to google sheet, producing better analysis of your company.

procedure

  • create a google sheet and google drive folder, and share them with datasyncer@syncforsimulated.iam.gserviceaccount.com
  • fill google form
  • upload your CSV files in a shared google drive folder, and your data will be updated within 4 hours in your google sheet (I know it's a long time but we don't have any server)
