guide to

sync for simulated

current version1.1

utilizing sync-for-simulated you can sync your stats from simcompanies.com to google sheet, producing better analysis of your company.

procedure

  • create a google sheet and google drive folder, and share them with datasyncer@syncforsimulated.iam.gserviceaccount.com .
  • fill google form.
  • upload your CSV files in a shared google drive folder, and your data will be updated within 4 hours in your google sheet (I know it's a long time but we don't have any server).

    • data-we-collect

  • we don't store any of your data from drive or sheet.
  • only data we store is one you fill in initial form, so we know where to look for CSVs and where to update sheet.
  • if you are still unsatisfied, all the data we have in open for everyone to see here.
  • in short we know following about you
       google sheet id (so we know which sheet to update)
       google drive folder id (so we know where to search for CSV files)
       company name (in case we need a contact with you)
      all data we have can't be used to get data from your drive or sheets (not even I can access)
